Aglio e Olio


Description

Very good recipe if you dont want to spend hours in the kitchen.
It is delicious, simple, fast and soo good.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • olive oil
  • chilli pepper
  • garlic
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water
  3. Heat olive oil
  4. Add chilli and garlic
  5. Add pasta water and toss
  6. Add drained pasta
  7. Mix with parsley
