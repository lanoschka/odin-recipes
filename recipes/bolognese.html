Bolognese


Description

The best and the most famous Italian sauce.
An absolute unit in the national dishes category. This recipe has zero haters.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • onion
  • bacon
  • garlic
  • ground beef
  • ground pork
  • tomatoes
  • mushrooms
  • carrots
  • stalk celery
  • dry red wine
  • chicken stock
  • spices
  • tagliatelle noodles

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables
  2. Put them into the dish with heated olive oil
  3. Add meat
  4. Add wine
  5. Add tomatoes
  6. Mix with spices and let it cook for 2 hours
  7. Serve sauce over hot pasta
