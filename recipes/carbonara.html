Carbonara


Description

Really simple and fast recipe.
It is so good and creamy.

Ingredients

  • guanciale
  • olive oil
  • spaghetti noodles
  • cheese
  • seasonings

Steps

  1. Cook the pork until crispy
  2. Boil noodles in salted water
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese with pepper
  4. Pour the mixture into pasta, stir until creamy
  5. Stir with the pork, then top with remaining cheese
